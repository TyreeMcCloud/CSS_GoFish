How to Play Go Fish

Go Fish is a simple and fun card game for 2-6 players. The goal of the game is to collect sets of four cards of the same rank. Here's how to play:

  1. Each player is dealt 5 cards (7 cards if only 2 players).
  2. The remaining cards are placed face down in the center to form the draw pile.
  3. Players take turns, starting with the player to the left of the dealer.
  4. On their turn, a player asks another player if they have a specific rank of card (e.g., "Do you have any 3s?").
  5. If the other player has one or more cards of the requested rank, they must give all of them to the asking player.
  6. If the player does not have any cards of the requested rank, they say "Go Fish!" and the asking player draws a card from the draw pile.
  7. If the drawn card is the rank they asked for, the player gets another turn. Otherwise, the next player takes their turn.
  8. Players form a "book" when they collect 4 cards of the same rank and place it face up in front of them.
  9. The game continues until all 13 books have been made.
  10. The player with the most books at the end of the game wins!
